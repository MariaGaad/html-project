Artificial Intelligence is a large computer science branch dedicated to the development of intelligent machines that are capable of carrying out tasks usually requiring human intelligence. AI is a multidisciplinary science, but advances in machine and deep learning create a paradigm shift in virtually every sector of the technology industry.
