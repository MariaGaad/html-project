  • Artificial intelligence generally falls under two broad categories:

    • Narrow AI:

  • This kind of artificial intelligence, often referred to as poor AI, works within a specific context and is a simulation of human intelligence. Narrow AI always concentrates on a mission and although these devices seem intelligent, they work under much more restrictions and limitations than even the most simple human intelligence.

    • Artificial General Intelligence (AGI):

  • The kind of artificial understanding we see in the films, such as Westworld robots or Star Trek data: The next generation, is AGI, sometimes referred to as the "Fortune of the IA." AGI is a generally intelligent machine, and like a human being, this intelligence can be used to solve all problems.
